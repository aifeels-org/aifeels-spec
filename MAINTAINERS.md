# Aifeels Maintainers

This document lists the current maintainers of the Aifeels specification.

## Role of Maintainers

Maintainers are responsible for:
- Reviewing and merging specification changes
- Approving or rejecting RFCs
- Ensuring spec integrity and consistency
- Guarding against scope creep and vendor bias
- Maintaining neutral, community-focused governance

## Becoming a Maintainer

Maintainers are added based on:
- Sustained high-quality contributions
- Deep understanding of the specification
- Demonstrated commitment to neutrality
- Community trust and respect

**Process:**
1. Existing maintainers nominate candidates
2. Community feedback period (14 days)
3. Consensus decision among existing maintainers

## Maintainer Expectations

### Time Commitment
- Review PRs and RFCs within 7 days
- Participate in governance discussions
- Monitor issues for spec ambiguities

### Neutrality Commitment
Maintainers MUST:
- Avoid vendor bias in decision-making
- Disclose conflicts of interest
- Prioritize specification quality over personal/company interests

### Decision Making
- Seek consensus when possible
- Document rationale for decisions
- Be open to community feedback
