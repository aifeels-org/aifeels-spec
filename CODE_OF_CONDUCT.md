# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Aifeels is a vendor-neutral, community-governed standard. We are committed to providing a welcoming and professional environment for all participants.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Respectful and constructive dialogue
- Technical focus and evidence-based arguments
- Assume good faith in others' contributions
- Welcome diverse perspectives

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Personal attacks or harassment
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Spam or off-topic content
- Sharing others' private information
- Vendor promotion or commercial solicitation

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all Aifeels spaces: GitHub repositories, discussions, issues, pull requests, and any other community channels.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to:

**Email:** governance@aifeels.org

Reports will be handled confidentially by the specification maintainers. We are committed to responding within 7 days.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:
1. **Warning** - First offense, minor violations
2. **Temporary ban** - Repeated or more serious violations (7-30 days)
3. **Permanent ban** - Severe violations or repeated offenses after temporary ban

Maintainers will document all enforcement actions and make them available upon request (with personal information redacted).

## Appeals

Decisions may be appealed by contacting governance@aifeels.org within 14 days of the enforcement action.
